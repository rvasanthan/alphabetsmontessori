Infants

Approx 10 Weeks to 18 Months

Our Montessori is quiet, cozy and comfortable for the children. Our classrooms are very secure and home like. Our parents are always welcome for an excursion to our school. We are very happy to walk you through the secure and cozy environment where every child could enjoy and embrace themselves.

The first and foremost, the ratio of children to the teachers in this schools is going to be three to one. There will be an allocated primary care giver to monitor and communicates the progress about accomplishments, tendencies and concerns to the parents in a very frequent manner.

We strongly believe that proper communications between our teachers and parents should happen frequently to provide the best possible care.

Providing successful development in these areas will improve the strength, coordination which are necessary to enjoy sports, dance and recreational activities.

"One test of the correctness of educational procedure is the happiness of the child."

-- Dr. Maria Montessori

What is our Focus?

Health & Safety

Just like ours, your highest priority is also to choose the safe and healthy environment for your child. We always keep that in mind and prepared this environment.

Respect for the child

We treat every child as an individual and to be well understood. Every child in deserves great respect and that's why it is one of our core values.

Freedom of Movement

Our classrooms are designed with this in mind to encourage the child to move freely in the prepared environment which would enrich their physical and cognitive development

Choice of Materials

It is always very important to use the correct age appropriate materials which can help the child grow and develop to his or her full potential making the most of those critical learning years.

Social Interaction

We help our children to interact and enhance with their peers in the classroom. Here in our school, we give more focus to the lay the foundation for interpersonal connections with the other children.

Communication to Parents

We firmly believe that consistent communication between the teachers and the parents contribute a great part in the intellectual development of children.

Toddlers

Approx 18 Months to 3 Years

We strongly believe that our children are benefiting the most in the environment which provides them the appropriate opportunities to explore their full potential and capabilities. Our team of certified teachers focus is to build their independence and provoke their curiosity of the toddlers which will enrich their love for learning throughout their life.

Our classrooms are specifically designed to provide a beautiful and nurturing environment. The materials and furniture are carefully chosen to meet your child's developmental needs.

We always firmly believe the philosophy of Montessori where we provide the freedom and independence to choose the materials they are willing to work and enabling them to explore their own interests. This is an important step in the life of our children to develop self confidence and discovering their own preferences and passion.

With this approach the toddlers will experience less frustration and an astonishing growth in a short span of time and also dramatic advancement in language, fine motor skills and problem solving.

"The greatest development is achieved during the first years of life, and therefore it is then that the greatest care should be taken. If this is done, then the child does not become a burden; he will reveal himself as the greatest marvel of nature."

-- Dr. Maria Montessori

Children House

Approx 3 Years to 6 Years

This is the stage where their curiosity transforms into inspiration, discovery.

Our multiage classrooms are designed specifically to meet the developmental needs of our children. It explores the full depth of the authentic Montessori education. Our classroom environment is carefully prepared where our young children can direct themselves and build confidence and social skills.

Our Preschool program teaches the real life skills like leadership and Mentorship, helping young children and practical life activities.

"A child who has become master of his acts through long and repeated exercises, and who has been encouraged by the pleasant and interesting activities in which he has been engaged, is a child filled with health and joy and remarkable for his calmness and discipline."

-- Dr. Maria Montessori

What is our Focus?

Health & Safety

Just like ours, your highest priority is also to choose the safe and healthy environment for your child. We always keep that in mind and prepared this environment.

Practical Life

We provide daily activities like pouring, cleaning, buttoning and zipping which develops co-ordination, self-esteem, independence and concentration

Language, Reading & Writing

Children will learn the letters by sight, feel and texture with many sensory materials. Lessons, Story reading and handwriting will be practiced.

Mathematics

We provide strong foundation and understanding of numerical concepts and operations at their own pace with an end goal to perform math functions abstractly.

History & Science

We develop the inspiration for a child's future interest to learn History, science and culture through natural objects, gardening and beautiful cultural photographs

Ethical Development

We provide innovative development in ethics, character and the necessary social skills of our children through puppets, games and theme based lessons.

Senses

In our authentic Montessori way, we make our children to become more logical and perceptive. We let them explore and experience textures, colors, sizes and shapes.

Physical Education

We have several periods of outdoor play with well equipped playgrounds and courts. This will develop the fine motor skills, fitness and activities.
